Hello!


My name is Joe Micucci, and I'm a tech professional based out of Indianapolis, IN whose

expertise lies primarily in client-facing support, service, and success roles.


I spent the first decade of my working career in any, and all, facets of customer service & support. You name it, I've heard someone complain about it.

That all changed back in September of 2021 when I enrolled at Eleven Fifty Acadamey and took part in their Software Development Course.

I ended up graduating in December of that same year, and started my journey in tech, a field that I'm genuinely passionate about.


Through my coding bootcamp, I learned that while I enjoyed having the technical knowledge of a coder,

I still enjoyed the human interraction part of working for a company. This led to me getting into the world of

customer success, which has allowed me to combine my client skills with my technical skills to be an expert liaison

between clients and the internal technical teams I have worked with since graduating.


One of my largest passions outside of work is basketball, and the best way I would describe myself in the work place is a team-oriented

point guard that is a facilitator on the court making sure those I am playing with are able to get to their spots. That way, when

the ball gets in their hands, it's in an advantageous situation. Thus, allowing us all to thrive together.
